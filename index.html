Plaster Belly Wraps

Preserving the beauty of pregnancy and the bond that continues beyond birth.

The Process

Created during the final weeks of pregnancy, each plaster belly wrap gently captures the unique form of a mother’s body at a fleeting moment in time. The result is a one-of-a-kind keepsake that honors both transformation and connection.

After Birth

Once your baby has arrived, the wrap becomes part of a post-birth photo session. Together, mother and child reconnect with the form that held life, creating imagery that bridges before and after — presence and memory.

Care & Intention

Every wrap is created with intention, respect, and care. The process is gentle, non-invasive, and centered entirely on the comfort and experience of the mother.
